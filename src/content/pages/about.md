## Who We Are
Bright Futures Network (BFN) is a Texas-based nonprofit dedicated to supporting K–12 immigrant students and their families as they navigate the U.S. education system.

## Our Mission
We empower immigrant students and families through tutoring, mentoring, college preparation support, and culturally responsive workshops that make school systems easier to understand and access.

## Our Vision
A future where every immigrant student, regardless of background or language barriers, feels confident, supported, and equipped to succeed academically and socially within the American education system.

## Our Values
- Inclusivity  
- Empathy  
- Education  
- Empowerment
