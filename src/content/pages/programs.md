## Programs That Meet Families Where They Are
BFN offers practical, culturally responsive support designed for immigrant students and families.

### School Orientation Workshops
Workshops that help families understand how schools work—grade levels, report cards, attendance expectations, communication with teachers, and available resources.

### Mentorship & Tutoring
One-on-one support for students including tutoring, goal-setting, progress check-ins, confidence building, and help with school routines.

### College Preparation Support
For high school students: college exploration, applications, scholarship support, and FAFSA/financial aid guidance.

### Parent Workshops & Community Education Nights
Sessions that help parents advocate for their children, communicate with schools, and build confidence navigating the system.
